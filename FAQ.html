FAQ

Where is the pantry located within the Wesley Foundation Building?

209 Manna Ministries is on the top floor of the building. Signs for 209 Manna Ministries will be visible from the building's entrance.

Who can apply to shop at the pantry?

Any Virginia Tech student may apply to become a customer at 209 Manna Ministries, and any students with dependent family members may apply to shop for their families as well.

How Do I apply to shop at the pantry?

Come to the pantry during open hours to fill out our quick survey.

How many items can I take from the pantry?

Each item in the pantry has a certain number of points (1-6 points). Each week, you are allotted 50 points.

How much does it cost to shop at the pantry?

Being a customer is free! Everything at the pantry is volunteer run and donation based, so customers are able to shop without worrying about the cost of goods.

How can I volunteer or donate items for the pantry?

Please contact Holly or Kelsey about volunteering or donating items (a list of items needed at the pantry can be found under the "donate" tab).

Since 209 Manna Ministries is located inside the building for the United Methodist Campus Ministry, do applicants need to be affiliated with the church in any way?

No! The pantry will welcome anyone and will not discriminate against anyone due to age, gender, sexuality, faith, race, favorite food, field of study, taste in music, etc.
